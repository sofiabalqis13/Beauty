About Us

Welcome to BeautyFinder, your number one source for all things beauty. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of makeup product.

Founded in 2024 by Kamelia Balqis, BeautyFinder has come a long way from its beginnings. When Kamelia first started out, their passion for beauty drove them to start their own beauty customization website.

Our mission is to help you find the best beauty products based on your needs and preferences.

With BeautyFinder, building your dream collection is easy. Save your favorite products by adding it to your wishlist and get inspired by new recommendations tailored just for you based on your favourite brands and budget.

Thank you for choosing BeautyFinder! We hope you enjoy the journey of discovering beauty products with us.
